Christmas Bar Crawl Challenge

The Nidec elves have set a fiendish list of tasks for this years bar crawl. There is a task for everyday of advent and the prizes will be glorious and reasonably priced.

The rules:

  • All tasks MUST be accompanied by an image.
  • You may not submit two images to the same task, the most recent submission will be taken.
  • The scoring will be one point per task.

Prizes:

  • Highest Scorer.
  • Best Image Overall.
  • Highest Jump (TASK 24)
